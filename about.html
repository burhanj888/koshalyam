KOSHALYAM When Devanshu Pandya and Burhanuddin Jinwala pursued higher education, they realized rural students often lagged behind their urban peers in technical skills and confidence due to limited resources, guidance, and opportunities. Having experienced similar challenges growing up, they felt a personal responsibility to address these barriers and ensure equal opportunities for all.

OUR MISSION

To bridge the gap in technical education by making learning accessible, engaging, and future ready for every student.

We experience success through the success of our students.

TECH SEEKHO In May 2023, Devanshu Pandya and Burhanuddin Jinwala launched TechSeekho under Koshalyam Learning Solutions Pvt. Ltd., aiming to bridge the educational gap for students in Tier 3, Tier 4 cities, and rural areas. By addressing outdated curricula, lack of skilled trainers, and language barriers, TechSeekho provides innovative learning solutions through workshops, seminars, and an AI-powered platform, equipping students with future-ready skills to thrive in a tech-driven world.
